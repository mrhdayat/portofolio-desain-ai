# Project Name: Oula Active - Studio Campaign

## Project Description
Oula is a women's activewear brand. They needed photos for their new studio collection. The goal was to step away from standard gym fitness shots and create something that looked like a high-end editorial while still showing off the fit and fabric of the clothes.

### Client Goals
The client wanted a clean, premium look that would appeal to their target demographic on Instagram and their e-commerce site. They specifically asked for images that felt natural but highly stylized, avoiding the sweaty, high-intensity gym aesthetic in favor of a clean studio vibe.

### Challenges
The main issue was lighting. We had to show the texture of the pink fabric without washing out the model's skin tone or making the colors look flat. We also needed a set of images that worked across different layouts—Instagram stories, square feed posts, and wide website banners.

### My Approach & Solution
I set up a studio shoot focusing on high-contrast lighting to highlight the fabric's details. We used a mix of tight detail shots and full-body poses to give the client plenty of options. 

For the final delivery, I edited the batch with a slightly muted, film-like color grade to give it a more premium, less "fitness-influencer" look. To solve the layout problem, I delivered a full suite of ready-to-post assets, cropping and arranging collages specifically for 1:1 (feed), 9:16 (stories/reels), and 16:9 (web banners). 

The client received a complete package they could use immediately across all their marketing channels.
