**Project Name**
AI-Generated High-End Fashion Campaign & Lookbook

**Project Description**
My client wanted to launch a new athletic wear collection but lacked the budget and timeline for a traditional, on-location photoshoot. Their goal was to produce a high-fashion, hyper-realistic lookbook that conveyed motion, premium quality, and consistent branding.

The main challenge was achieving photo-realism without the "plastic" look common in AI generation, especially concerning fabric physics and consistent facial features across different poses.

To solve this, I built a custom AI image generation pipeline specifically tuned for fashion photography. I utilized strict control networks to preserve the original garment details while generating dynamic poses and studio-quality lighting. Finally, I applied professional color grading and editorial typography to package the raw AI outputs into a ready-to-publish digital brand campaign. The client received a premium lookbook at a fraction of a traditional agency's cost.
